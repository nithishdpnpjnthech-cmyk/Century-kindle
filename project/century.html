Overview Century Codename Winning Kind At Attur, Yelahanka, North Bangalore

Century Codename Winning Kind is a premium residential apartment project by Century Group, strategically located along Attur Main Road in Attur Layout, Yelahanka New Town, North Bangalore. At its core, Century Attur features thoughtfully designed 2, 3 & 4 BHK apartments with sizes, which cater to diverse lifestyle requirements—from smaller family homes to spacious residences for larger households. The project is structured across multiple high-rise towers with around 800 total homes set within a scenic landscape that prioritizes open spaces, greenery, and community-centric design. Century Attur is positioned in Yelahanka New Town, an area known for its excellent connectivity and developing infrastructure. Residents can enjoy smooth commutes to key Bangalore hubs like Kempegowda International Airport, Manyata Tech Park, and Hebbal, thanks to the proximity to major roads such as Attur Main Road, Bellary Road, and the Outer Ring Road.
